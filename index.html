About

ALBY | Fashion and Apparel

ALBY stands for Aemy Lim Bee Yuen, which is the brand owner's full birth-name in Singapore. She is also known as Mrs Rasmussen Aemy, taking after her husband‘s name.

ALBY is an Asian fashion designer inspired by Nordic haute couture. She developed her own ‘classic with a twist’ style. The ALBY brand story is simple: Aemy Lim is an experienced General Sales Manager with a demonstrated history of working 15 years with the Lufthansa Group in Asia/China. As a veteran in the China tourism market, she was awarded the “Female Leader of the Year 2016” by the China National Tourism Board in Beijing. She believed travelling in this world is the best education she can ever have, and meeting new people while travelling is also lots of fun and an excellent opportunity to make some long-lasting friendships and cultures exchanged.

A career switch isn’t something that most would consider; nonetheless, Aemy concluded that she wanted to try something that she is passionate about, something that will create sparks and motivation in her life. Having spent over 12 years in Greater China, she has amassed a wide range of networks within Greater China that inspired her to go into fashion. Over the years, the style lesson she’d learnt was; one needs to know what you look good in. Sometimes we’re so distracted by trends or by an outfit, but ultimately you need to find what works for you. As a working woman, Aemy often goes from office to an evening function; she discovers that she relies a lot on transitional pieces that can go from day to night. She also gravitates extremes: she’ll either look for something very classic and timeless. Alternatively, go in the opposite direction that’s really whimsical and fun. Her visions are to create a fashion and apparel line that is relatable and wearable to “everyday working women” but also comfortable, fashionable, modern, edgy!

The brand story is about a well-travelled Asian lady used "East meets West", modern Danish and Swedish design as the inspiration and starting off point for the ALBY Summer/Falls 2018 collection. She was particularly drawn to the contrast between linear shapes and more fluid and round design sensibility, which she has explored through the use of vintage lady images fabric, plaids, and textures and seaming details. –“Her inspiration was in creating a comfortable, serenity moment for all confident modern successful women." ALBY is a small tailor-made boutique collection, and it’s a story that reflects simplicity with a timeless classic.

On a parallel, ALBY also set up a partnership with young talented designers [refer next page about design partnership] focusing on home decor, fashion & apparel products that will sell in Asia/China.

ALBY | Design Partnership

With more than 12 years of experience in sales throughout China. ALBY is a marketplace for upcoming new designers to introduce their new & exciting creations, products and concepts that can sell in China/Asia.

The commercial processes for an individual designer are overwhelming and resource-consuming, and usually complicated. Now ALBY makes it possible for designers to get their products and names into a brand new market, for the benefit of new designers as well as ALBY itself. We assist in taking care of designer products launch from financing, sourcing and production to final sales to retail stores/consumers
